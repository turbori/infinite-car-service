LGA Transfer FAQs

How long does it take to get from Long Island to LaGuardia?

From most Nassau County locations, the drive to LGA takes 10-20 minutes depending on traffic. From Suffolk County, expect 30-45 minutes. LGA is the closest NYC airport to Long Island.

Do you track flights at LaGuardia?

Yes, we monitor all LGA flights in real-time and automatically adjust your pickup time for delays or early arrivals. You'll never miss your ride.

Which terminals do you serve at LaGuardia?

We serve all LaGuardia terminals: Terminal A, Terminal B (with Meet & Greet available), and Terminal C (with Meet & Greet available). Simply tell us your airline and we'll know exactly where to meet you.

Can I book round-trips to LaGuardia?

Absolutely! We offer discounted rates for round-trip bookings. Many business travelers book both their outbound and return trips at once for convenience and savings.
