JFK Transfer FAQs

What if my JFK flight is delayed?

We track your flight in real-time and automatically adjust your pickup time. Your chauffeur will be waiting whenever you land ‚Äî no extra charges for delays.

How does meet & greet at JFK work?

For $50, your chauffeur meets you inside the terminal at baggage claim with a personalized sign. They assist with luggage and escort you directly to the vehicle ‚Äî no AirTrain or shuttle buses required.

How early should I book my JFK transfer?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for best availability. For early morning flights or holiday travel, 48-72 hours is ideal.

Do you serve all JFK terminals?

Yes, we provide service to all JFK terminals (1, 4, 5, 7, and 8). Just provide your airline and we'll know exactly where to meet you.
