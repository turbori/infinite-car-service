Iconic NYC Attractions

Must-see destinations for every NYC visitor

Times Square

The bustling heart of NYC, featuring bright lights, Broadway theaters, shopping, and dining. Perfect for first-time visitors and tourists.

Best visited: Evening for full light experience
Drop-off: Broadway & 42nd Street area

Central Park

Manhattan's green oasis offering peaceful walks, boat rides, zoo visits, and beautiful landscapes year-round.

Best visited: Morning or afternoon
Multiple entrance points available

Statue of Liberty

America's symbol of freedom. We'll take you to Battery Park for ferry departures to Liberty Island and Ellis Island.

Plan full day trip with ferry rides
Drop-off: Battery Park ferry terminal

Brooklyn Bridge

Historic bridge offering stunning views of Manhattan skyline. Walk across for unforgettable photo opportunities.

Best views: Morning or sunset
Drop-off: City Hall area or DUMBO

Empire State Building

Iconic Art Deco skyscraper with observation decks offering 360-degree views of NYC from the 86th and 102nd floors.

Visit at sunset for day-to-night views
Drop-off: 34th Street entrance

The High Line

Elevated park built on former railway tracks, featuring gardens, art installations, and unique city views.

Best visited: Afternoon for perfect lighting
Multiple access points in Chelsea
