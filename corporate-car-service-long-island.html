Corporate Account FAQs

How does corporate billing work?

You receive one consolidated monthly statement with detailed trip breakdowns for all your company's rides. Each trip includes date, time, passenger name, pickup/drop-off locations, and cost. Perfect for expense management and accounting.

Do you offer priority booking for corporate accounts?

Yes! Corporate accounts receive priority booking and guaranteed availability, even during peak times like holidays or major NYC events. You'll also have a dedicated dispatch contact who knows your company's preferences.

Can we set up recurring schedules for executives?

Absolutely! Many of our corporate clients have weekly or bi-weekly schedules for their executives (e.g., Monday morning LGA flights, Thursday Manhattan meetings). We can assign consistent chauffeurs and handle all scheduling automatically.

What's included in a corporate account?

Corporate accounts include: monthly consolidated billing, priority booking, dedicated dispatch contact, preferred chauffeur assignments, last-minute booking flexibility, usage reports, and 10,000+ support for all your executives and employees.

How quickly can we set up a corporate account?

Setup is quick and easy ‚Äî usually within 24 hours. We'll work with your finance/HR team to establish billing terms, authorized users, and any special requirements your company has.
