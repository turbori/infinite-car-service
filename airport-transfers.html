Know Before You Go: Airport Meeting Points

JFK Construction Update

JFK's $19 billion renovation has made standard pickups extremely complicated. Most terminals now require a lengthy AirTrain journey to remote lots with multiple transfers, crowded shuttles, and significant walking with luggage. Our $50 Inside Terminal Service eliminates this hassle completely!
