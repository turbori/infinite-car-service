Terminal-Specific Meeting Points

Detailed instructions for each airport terminal

JFK Airport (JFK)

Important: JFK's $19 billion construction project has made pickup extremely complicated. Most terminals now require a lengthy AirTrain journey to remote lots, with multiple transfers, crowded shuttle buses, and significant walking with luggage. Plus, there's an additional $2.50 PANYNJ Airport Access Fee.
Skip this exhausting process entirely with our $50 Inside Terminal Service!

Standard Service Meeting Points:

Terminal 1

Take AirTrain to Howard Beach/Ride App Pick Up Lot. This involves navigating crowded terminals, waiting for AirTrain, multiple stops, and walking through remote parking areas with your luggage.

Terminal 4 Premium Available

ALL DAY: Take mandatory shuttle bus from Terminal 4 to Lot 66 (runs every 1-2 minutes, but adds significant time with luggage, crowds, and multiple transfers). This inconvenient process is required at all times due to ongoing construction.

Premium Inside Service Available
Terminal 5 Premium Available

Take AirTrain to Howard Beach/Ride App Pick Up Lot. The journey involves navigating construction areas, crowded AirTrain cars, potential delays, and then finding your way through the remote parking lot with heavy luggage.

Premium Inside Service Available
Terminal 7

Take AirTrain to Howard Beach/Ride App Pick Up Lot. Terminal 7 has significant roadway changes due to construction, making the already complex AirTrain process even more confusing and time-consuming with heavy bags.

Terminal 8 Premium Available

Take AirTrain to Howard Beach/Ride App Pick Up Lot. Terminal 8 access has been redirected due to construction, adding complexity to an already challenging journey involving multiple transfers and walking through remote lots with luggage.

Premium Inside Service Available

LaGuardia (LGA)

Good News: Premium Inside Terminal Service available at all LGA terminals for $50.

Terminal Meeting Points:

Terminal A Premium Available

The passenger pick-up area for Car Services and App-based Rides is located directly outside of, and across the street from, Terminal A.

Premium Inside Service Available
Terminal B Premium Available

The Car Service and App-Based Ride pick-up area is located on Level 2 of the Terminal B Parking Garage, located directly adjacent to Terminal B. Please follow the "Car Services" signs in Terminal B to the pick-up area and contact your car service/app-based ride provider when you reach the pick-up area.

Specific Meeting Point: Between signs K1 and K2 in the parking garage.

Premium Inside Service Available
Terminal C Premium Available

The Car Service and App-Based Ride pick-up areas are located at Terminal C arrivals (ground) level. Terminal C pick-up is at the arrivals inner lane. Please follow the "Car Services" signs in Terminal C to the pick-up area and contact your car service/app-based ride provider when you reach the respective pick-up area.

Specific Meeting Point: Between pillars 12A and 12B at arrivals level.

Premium Inside Service Available

Standard Pickup Service

General information and post-landing instructions for all airports

Standard Pickup Information

Your chauffeur will be waiting with a professional sign displaying your name. Please look for the "Infinite Car Service" logo and your name clearly displayed.

Flight Monitoring: We track your flight status and adjust pickup times accordingly.
Contact Information: You'll receive your chauffeur's contact details before arrival.
Luggage Assistance: Your chauffeur will help with all your luggage.
Complimentary Wait: 60 minutes domestic, 90 minutes international flights.

After You Land

You'll receive this text message after landing:

"Additional Instructions: Your chauffeur is parked 5 minutes away at the waiting lot. Please describe your outfit and belongings so your chauffeur can spot you quickly. If you have checked bags, please let us know once the bags start dropping on the carousel. Security will not allow the chauffeur to stay at the terminal for too long, so please keep us updated with your status. Thank you."

Important Points:

  • Chauffeur parks 5 minutes away in waiting lot
  • Describe your outfit for quick identification
  • Notify when bags appear on carousel
  • Security limits terminal wait times
  • Keep us updated on your status
