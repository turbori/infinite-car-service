Driven by Purpose. Built on Trust.

Founded in 2005

Infinite Car Service was born with a clear mission — to provide Long Island with the most dependable and luxurious transportation available.

What began as a small, family-run operation has grown into Long Island's most trusted black car service, known for punctuality, professionalism, and an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction. Every ride is our chance to exceed expectations.

Meet Rafi – Our Founder

Rafi started Infinite Car Service with one goal: raise the standard of transportation in New York.

With 19+ years of hands-on experience, he personally oversees every part of the business — from hiring to client service — ensuring every trip reflects the brand's values: trust, elegance, and care. His passion for delivering an elite customer experience is the heart of Infinite Car Service.
