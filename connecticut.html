Connecticut Service FAQs

How long does it take to get from Greenwich to Manhattan?

Travel time from Greenwich to Midtown Manhattan typically ranges from 45-75 minutes depending on traffic. Early morning trips (before 7am) are usually faster, while rush hour can add time. We always factor in traffic conditions when planning your pickup time.

Do you offer regular commuter service?

Yes! Many of our Connecticut clients use us for regular NYC commutes. We offer flexible scheduling for recurring trips, and you can book the same chauffeur for consistency. Ask about our corporate account options for frequent travelers.

Which airports do you serve from Connecticut?

We provide service to all major airports: JFK, LaGuardia, Newark (EWR), Westchester County (HPN), and Teterboro (private jets). We also serve Bradley International Airport (BDL) for Hartford-area clients.

What if my flight is delayed?

We track all flights in real-time and automatically adjust for delays. Your chauffeur will be there when you land, not when you were originally scheduled. We include 60 minutes of complimentary wait time for all airport pickups.

Can I book a round-trip for the same day?

Absolutely! Same-day round trips are very popular for Connecticut clients going to NYC for meetings or events. You can book a specific return time or keep it flexible ‚Äî just text your chauffeur when you're ready to head back.
