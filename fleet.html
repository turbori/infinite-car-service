Child Safety Seats

We offer luxury transportation with safety in mind for your little ones — all child seats are cleaned and inspected before each ride.

Available Options

  • Rear-Facing Infant Seat
    5–35 lbs
  • Forward-Facing Toddler Seat
    20–65 lbs
  • Booster Seat
    40–100 lbs

NY State Regulations

  • Under 2 years old:
    Rear-facing seat
  • 2–4 years old:
    Forward-facing with harness
  • 4–8 years old:
    Booster seat unless over 4'9" or 100 lbs
  • 8+ years old:
    Seatbelt if height/weight permits

We're happy to help you select the right seat based on your child's age, height, and weight.
