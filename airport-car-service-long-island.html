Frequently Asked Questions

Do you track my flight for delays?

Yes, we monitor all flights in real-time using advanced tracking technology. If your flight is delayed, we automatically adjust your pickup time ‚Äî no need to call us.

Can we set up a recurring airport schedule for executives?

Absolutely! Many of our clients have weekly or bi-weekly airport schedules. We can assign the same chauffeur and customize the experience to your preferences.

Do you offer corporate billing or accounts?

Yes, we provide corporate accounts with monthly billing, itemized invoices, and dedicated support. Perfect for companies booking regular travel.

What areas of Long Island do you serve?

We serve all of Long Island including Nassau County, Suffolk County, the Hamptons, North Fork, and everywhere in between.

Can I book round-trip airport transfers?

Yes! We offer discounted rates for round-trip bookings. Many business travelers book their entire trip (pickup and return) in one reservation.
